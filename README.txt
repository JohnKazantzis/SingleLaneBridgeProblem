A simple solution to the single lane bridge problem

> Coded in Python 2.7

> Problem:
  Two types of cars (red and blue) arrive at a single lane bridge. Each car is represented by a new thread. 
  At any given moment only one car can arrive or cross the bridge. 
  - Random cars arrive at random times
  - There must be a sense of justice
  - Its car increments the shared variable 3 times. One when it arrives at the bridge, one when its crossing
    the bridge and one when it just crossed the bridge.
    
> Usage: python SingleLaneBridge.py [Number Of Cars]

> Available options:
  -h , --help   Prints the usage string
